# Business Context Questions

Questions organized by output file. Ask ONE at a time, in order.

---

## File 1: identity.md

**Q1.1** What's your company name and website?

**Q1.2** What industry or category are you in?
- Examples: B2B SaaS, MarTech, FinTech, DevTools, HR Tech

**Q1.3** What's your business model?
- A. SaaS (subscription software)
- B. Marketplace
- C. Services
- D. Hybrid

**Q1.4** What stage is your company at?
- A. Pre-PMF (still finding product-market fit)
- B. Post-PMF (proven fit, growing)
- C. Scale (rapid growth phase)
- D. Enterprise (mature, established)

**Q1.5** What countries/regions do you operate in?

**Q1.6** What are your primary languages for marketing?

---

## File 2: icp.md

**Q2.1** What industries do your ideal customers operate in?

**Q2.2** What company sizes do you target?
- Examples: 11-50, 51-200, 201-1000, 1000+

**Q2.3** What regions/geographies do you target?

**Q2.4** Who are your buyer personas? (Job titles/functions)

**Q2.5** What seniority levels do you target?

**Q2.6** Who is the decision-maker vs influencer vs user?

**Q2.7** What types of customers are a bad fit?

## File 3: product.md

**Q3.1** What is your core product or service? (One sentence)

**Q3.2** What are the top 3-5 use cases?

**Q3.3** What's your pricing model?
- A. Subscription
- B. Usage-based
- C. One-time
- D. Hybrid

**Q3.4** What's your sales motion?
- A. Self-serve
- B. Sales-assisted
- C. Enterprise

**Q3.5** Do you offer a free trial? (Duration?)

**Q3.6** Is a demo typically required?

**Q3.7** What's your typical sales cycle length?

**Q3.8** What are the top 3 problems your product solves?

**Q3.9** What happens if customers DON'T solve these problems?

**Q3.10** How does your product impact revenue/cost/risk/time?

**Q3.11** Do you have quantified outcomes from customers?
